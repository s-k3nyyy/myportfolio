PIZZA

recipe

    Ingredients required

  • pizza dough
  • pepperoni
  • cheese
  • sausage
  • mushrooms
  • onions
  • tomatoes
  • peppers

    instructions for preparations

  1. Preheat your oven to the temperature specified in your pizza dough recipe or on the store-bought dough packaging.
  2. Prepare your pizza dough. If using store-bought dough, follow the instructions on the packaging. If making your own, roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Cut the pizza dough into 12-inch slices.
  4. Place the rolled-out dough on a pizza pan or baking sheet lined with parchment paper or sprinkled with cornmeal or flour to prevent sticking.
  5. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.
  6. Bake the pizza for 15-18 minutes, or until the pizza is tender.
  7. Remove the dough from the oven and set aside.
  8. Sprinkle cheese evenly over the sauce. Use as much or as little cheese as you like, depending on your preference.
  9. Sprinkle mushrooms evenly over the sauce. Use as much or as little mushrooms as you like, depending on your preference.
  10. Carefully transfer the pizza to the preheated oven and bake according to the dough recipe or package instructions, typically for about 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  11. Once the pizza is done, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
  12. Enjoy your delicious homemade pizza!
